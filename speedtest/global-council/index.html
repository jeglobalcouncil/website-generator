Global Council

Who we are

The Junior Enterprise Global Council is the International Organization that represents the Junior Enterprise Movement Worldwide.

We are currently composed by 8 confederations which represent a total of 20 countries, all united in the Council with the following mission:

To be a truly global and connected movement, by improving our results via increased cooperation.

Since the confederations already represent JEs on a national or continental level, our work is focused on global aspects of the network.

Our history

Even though the JE Movement exists since 1967, a global cooperation only started to be written in 2006 with the cooperation agreement signed between the European and Brazilian confederations, which led to the creation of the Junior Enterprise World Conference (JEWC).

In JEWC 2016, the second-ever JE Global Forum was held. National leaders united to discuss the next steps for the international network and the main resolution was the creation of the Global Council as a collaboration platform within the founder members.

What we do

Alignment

Promotion of the International Standards, ensuring JEs follow the JE concept.

Production of the Global Report, giving to the JE network insights about where we are and where we're going.

Enlargement

Partnerships and programs to stimulate the creation of JEs.

Support for JE and Confederation initiatives in more than 20 countries around the world.

Development

Support for the newer confederations.

Connection programs to increase collaboration in the network.

Communication initiatives to promote global knowledge.
